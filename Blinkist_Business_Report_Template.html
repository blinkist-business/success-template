Choose your partnership depth

There's no wrong answer — pick the level that matches your bandwidth

How much your organisation gets out of Blinkist depends on two things: how much commitment your admin team and exec sponsor bring to driving the programme, and how broadly Blinkist is wired into your organisation — seat penetration, LMS integration, and the number of touchpoints live. We pick the right depth together at kickoff and revisit it every QBR. Organic is a perfectly valid choice if light-touch is what fits — many customers run it that way. A strategic partnership is what you reach for when you want measurable, programme-grade impact.
